Big Ideas for Health and Science

The following sections detail my two big ideas for data science. The first idea, A Kernel of Health, seeks to find possible causes for idiopathic medical conditions by harnessing networks of patients to reveal patterns that have thus far gone undetected. The second idea, A Speaker for Crows, aims to leverage available recorded calls by crows and other corvids to enable bird identification without the need for tagging.
